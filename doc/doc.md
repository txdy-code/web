### 概念文案（首次上架必填）
GraceTrail keeps discipleship quiet, practical, and steady. Check your mood, pray through curated words, stay anchored in Scripture, and finish with one focused quiz question.

### 产品描述（首次上架必填）
GraceTrail is a compact spiritual companion for believers who want a measurable daily rhythm built around four pillars: Mood, Prayer, Bible, and Quiz. Each module works offline-first, appears in eight languages, and tells you what to do next. Record how you feel, pray through curated words, read the next chapter, test yourself, and celebrate small wins without distractions.

### Key Features
1. **Mood & Today Dashboard**
   - Weekly 7-day completion rings highlight mood, prayer, and quiz tasks plus streaks, totals, and a color mood semi-circle.
   - Five mood tiers (Awesome→Terrible) unlock paired verses, reference notes, and a typewriter animation for calm reflection.

2. **Prayer Flow**
   - Time-aware morning (00:00–16:59) and evening (17:00–23:59) prayers refresh automatically when the day shifts or the app resumes.
   - Each prayer bundles verse, inspiration, and Amen confirmation with a dedicated completion screen so progress is provable even offline.

3. **Bible Study Hub**
   - Full 66-book Old/New Testament tabs with horizontal chapter navigation, Mark as Read, and Next Chapter actions that remember scroll position.
   - Long-press highlights with custom colors, filterable highlight list, precise jump, 500 ms search, and reader controls for font, line spacing, background, text, and theme.

4. **Quiz Journey**
   - 500 curated questions delivered in three-question micro-levels; each item shuffles four answers and cites Scripture for context reinforcement.
   - Resurrection options (Keep Learning / Give Up), three-star scoring, confetti plus Lottie celebration, and insights tracking for accuracy and streaks.

**Plus: Profile & Reliability**
- Profile tab shows avatar, totals for prayers/moods/read chapters, and quick actions for Settings, Theme, Language, and Privacy & Terms.
- One-time unzip of Bible/Prayer/Quiz bundles, Drift-backed tables, and provider-based lifecycle management keep the experience offline-ready and smooth on low-end devices.

### Why GraceTrail?
- Replaces scattered devotion habits with a simple loop: check in, pray, read, test, repeat.
- Keeps you accountable through streak data, quiz insights, and recorded prayers while staying private and offline.
- Serves global believers with eight languages, flexible themes, and permission guidance that just works.
